The Deal

What even is a cuore pazzo?

It means crazy heart — and now it's the thing in your hand. We stuff bold, unapologetically vegetarian fillings into a mini sourdough pocket, crimp it shut, and fry it into a golden heart. No meat. No cutlery. No chill.

Three days of fermentation you'll never see. You'll taste it.

01

100% Vegetarian

Every cuore, every time. Plants have never tasted this unhinged.

02

Sealed & crimped

Sprouted whole-grain sourdough, cold-fermented 72 hours, sealed and fried into a golden heart. The crimped edge is the signature.

03

Bold names, bolder flavours

FUHGETABOUTIT. YES IYAH. If the name makes you laugh, the flavour'll make you stay.

Pay it forward

The People's Cuore

A warm handful for someone who needs one. They pay $1. You pay it forward. They walk away with two or three cuores — no questions, no catch — just something warm and a little faith in people.

This one's about you

Your Story

It's noon on a Saturday. You're hungry, you want something good — and somehow that's the hard part.

you deserve a better lunch crazy heart, serious snack you deserve a better lunch crazy heart, serious snack
The part nobody enjoys

Sound like a lunch you've had?

“meh.”

Vegetarian = boring

You go meat-free and the menu shrinks to a sad side salad and a wrap that tastes like the fridge.

“what's in it?”

Mystery ingredients

Bleached flour, fillers, things you can't pronounce. You wanted lunch, not a chemistry quiz.

“same again?”

Zero personality

Every spot blurs together. Nothing surprises you, nothing's worth telling a friend about.

Then you find us

How it goes with cuore pazzo

01

You spot the crazy heart

A grinning mascot, a name like FUHGETABOUTIT, and the smell of fresh sourdough. You're curious before you've even read the menu.

02

You order without compromise

Every option is vegetarian, so there's no “what can I actually eat here?” math. Just pick the one that sounds the most unhinged.

03

You take the first bite

A little sealed heart of 72-hour sprouted sourdough — crisp crimped edge, warm molten middle, real ingredients. Bold flavour that lives up to the bold name.

04

You become a regular

You bring a friend. You pay forward a People's Cuore. You start counting down to the next weekend drop. Fatto con amore, returned.

Lunch should make you feel something

We started Cuore Pazzo because “vegetarian” shouldn't mean settling, and a snack shouldn't be forgettable. Real sourdough, wild flavour, a sealed little heart, and a cuore for whoever needs one. That's the whole idea.
